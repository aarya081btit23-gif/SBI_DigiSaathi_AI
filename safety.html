Guardrails & Compliance Layer

Safe digital adoption by design.

The prototype includes a safety layer so the AI educates users without collecting secrets or giving risky financial advice.

🚫

No OTP/PIN requests

The assistant never asks for OTP, PIN, password, CVV or full card details.

🏦

Official channels only

Users are guided to official SBI apps, website, branch or verified support only.

⚖

Advice boundary

DigiSaathi explains products and adoption steps, but avoids unauthorized financial advisory claims.

Safety checklist shown to user

CheckStatus
Do not share OTP/PINAlways visible warning
Use official YONO SBI / SBI websiteShown before digital action
Verify beneficiary and amountBefore payments/transfers
Escalate confusionBranch/support fallback

Business value

By reducing fear and confusion, SBI DigiSaathi AI can increase digital product adoption, improve engagement, reduce branch/call-center dependency, and create better cross-sell opportunities for deposits, insurance, loans and investment education.


✓This safety-first approach makes the prototype more credible for banking and BFSI hackathon evaluation.
