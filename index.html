Problem

Customers want digital banking, but need guided confidence.

Many first-time, elderly and less digitally confident users avoid services like UPI, mobile banking, bill payments, deposits and insurance because the journey feels complex or unsafe.

?

Low awareness

Customers often do not know which digital service fits their goal: saving, paying bills, investing or protection.

🛡

Fear of fraud

Users hesitate because of OTP/PIN scams and confusion between official and fake banking channels.

↗

Drop-offs

Even interested customers leave midway when forms, terms or app navigation becomes difficult.

Solution

SBI DigiSaathi AI

A contextual, adaptive and safety-aware AI assistant that converts a customer goal into a guided digital banking journey.


Launch AI Chat

Conversational onboarding

Asks simple questions and understands intent, digital literacy, language preference and goal.

Personalized recommendation

Suggests services like YONO, UPI, RD/FD, bill payment, insurance or investment education.

Safe execution guide

Gives official-channel guidance and never asks for OTP, PIN, password or card secrets.

Process Flow / Architecture

Agentic AI flow from user goal to digital adoption

1User Input

Text/voice query such as “I want to save ₹1000 every month.”

2AI Understanding

Detects intent, goal, preferred language and digital comfort level.

3Decision Engine

Chooses the best next journey: savings, payments, insurance or onboarding.

4Recommendation

Shows suitable SBI digital service with benefits and risk/safety notes.

5Guidance

Provides step-by-step instructions, reminders and feedback loop.
